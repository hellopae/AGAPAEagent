## Blockers
*(Must fix before publish)*

**1. Factual misattribution — Section 3, ด้านที่ 4**

Sentence: *"ePS Radius 13.0 และ HP Nio ซึ่งเป็น AI agent บนระบบ PrintOS ช่วย production insights แบบ real-time"*

The relative clause "ซึ่งเป็น AI agent บนระบบ PrintOS" attaches grammatically to both "ePS Radius 13.0 AND HP Nio," making it appear both products are AI agents on HP's PrintOS platform. This is factually wrong. ePS Radius 13.0 is an EFI MIS/ERP system — a separate product from a separate company. Only HP Nio is an AI agent on PrintOS.

Fix: Split into two distinct descriptions. Example structure: describe HP Nio as the AI agent on PrintOS first, then describe ePS Radius 13.0 separately as an MIS-side tool. The two must not be grouped under a single "ซึ่งเป็น" clause.

## Warnings
*(Should fix before publish)*

**1. CTA lacks a specific contact channel**

The closing line — *"ทักมาได้เลย ผมยินดีแลกเปลี่ยนมุมมองกัน"* — invites conversation but provides no channel. For web publication on Claudy, this needs a LINE ID, Facebook page handle, email, or explicit link. Without it, readers who want to follow up hit a dead end.

**2. Coined term "ปีแตกหักออก" in headline carries unintended negative register**

"แตกหัก" in standard Thai means a decisive negative break (a failed relationship, a severed partnership). Using it to mean "breakout" is a deliberate creative choice that the author explains inside the article — but the headline lands before readers reach that explanation. On LINE shares or Facebook previews, the headline appears alone. Consider adding a parenthetical in the headline (e.g., "ปีแตกหักออก — breakout year") or front-loading the explanation.
